## Crisis Management

**How to Handle:**
1. **Negative Comments:** Respond professionally, offer to help via DM
2. **Spam:** Delete and block immediately
3. **Incorrect Information:** Acknowledge, correct, thank commenter
4. **User Complaints:** Empathize, investigate, resolve publicly or privately

**Response Templates:**
```
"Thanks for bringing this to our attention! We're looking into it and will DM you shortly."

"We appreciate your feedback! This helps us improve. What specifically can we do better?"

"Apologies for the inconvenience! Please DM us your details so we can resolve this."
```
